# Off Grid AI — Mission & Vision

## Mission
Turn every organization into an **intelligent enterprise**, running on AI that is reliable, secure, and compliant by default.
Put that AI in the hands of **every employee, not just engineers** — inside a governed sandbox where nothing leaks and nothing breaks.
Let anyone describe what they need in plain language and get a working, governed workflow, with policy, guardrails, and audit built in.

## Vision
Real change reaches the **population** through the enterprises that serve it — so we make those enterprises intelligent.
Intelligence woven into how they actually operate: every team, every decision, compounding on one governed spine.
Achieved by **working with the systems they already run, as-is** — without asking them to change a thing; we meet their data, tools, and infrastructure where it is.
